Welcome to pyDTCWT's documentation!
===================================

Introduction
------------

pyDTCWT is a python implementation of the Dual-Tree Complex Wavelet Transform
[Kingsbury99]_ [Selesnick05]_.

.. [Kingsbury99] N G Kingsbury "Image Processing with Complex Wavelets", 
   *Phil. Trans. Royal Society London A*, September 1999, 
   on a Discussion Meeting on "Wavelets: the key to intermittent 
   information?", London, February 24-25, 1999.

.. [Selesnick05] I W Selesnick, R G Baraniuk, and N G Kingsbury, 
   "The Dual-Tree Complex Wavelet Transform," *IEEE Signal Processing 
   Magazine*, vol 22, no 6, pp 123-151, Nov. 2005.
